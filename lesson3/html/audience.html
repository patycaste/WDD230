Target Audience

The target audience for this page are people that are looking for a place to go out on a weekend or a place for vacation, people who are looking for a colonial place to visit, delicious food, and a place where to rest from the sounds of the city.

Person 1 - Steven Larsen

30-year-old man, a professional that is looking for a place to go for vacation. He likes to make tourism, try new gastronomy and know many places. He uses to look for places to go for vacation through his cellphone or tablet in his free time. He looks for a warm, colonial place to visit.

Person 2 - Susana Ibarra

A retired woman in her 60's. She accesses the page from her laptop. What she looks for in a place is to find delicious food, souvenir stores and have a romantic date with her husband in a lovely place.
